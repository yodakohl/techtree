Huygens-Bruce Marine Clock Prototypes

Purpose-built pendulum sea clocks developed by Christiaan Huygens, Alexander Bruce, and Severyn Oosterwijck for 1662 longitude trials, an early but unsuccessful mechanical timekeeping approach at sea.
